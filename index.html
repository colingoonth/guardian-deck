The gap in the market

Every solution checks the number.
None of them listen to the caller.

What exists today

T-Mobile Scam Shield, RoboKiller, Truecaller, Nomorobo — all check if the phone number is in a database.

A scammer spoofs a new number and every one lets it through.

No real-time analysis. No AI. No understanding of what the caller says.

What's missing

A system that doesn't just check who's calling — but listens to what they're saying.

Something that intercepts the call and uses AI to determine if it's a scam in real time — before the phone ever rings.

Databases are reactive. Scammers are proactive.
